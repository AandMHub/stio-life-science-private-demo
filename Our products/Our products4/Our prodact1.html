    Ropivacaine hydrochloride 2mg/ml Solution for infusion

    1. Qualitative and Quantitative Composition:

    Each ml contains 2 mg Ropivacaine hydrochloride.

    Each 100 ml-bag contains Ropivacaine HCl monohydrate 211.4 mg (Eq. to Ropivacaine HCl 1mg) = 200 mg.

    2. Pharmaceutical Form:

    Solution for infusion.

    Clear, colorless solution.

    3. Clinical Particulars:

    3.1. Therapeutic indications:

    Ropivacaine SLS 2 mg/ml is indicated for acute pain management.

    In adults and adolescents over 12 years of age for:

    – Continuous epidural infusion during postoperative pain or labor pain;

    – Field blocks;

    – Continuous peripheral nerve block via a continuous infusion, e.g. postoperative pain management.

    In infants from 1 year and children aged 12 years or less (pre- and postoperative):
    – Single and continuous peripheral nerve block.

    In neonates, infants and children aged 12 years or less for (pre- and postoperative):
    – Caudal epidural block.
    – Continuous epidural infusion.

    3.2. Posology and method of administration:

    Table 1: Adults and adolescents over 12 years of age:

    Method of administration: perineural and epidural administration by infusion

    containing Ropivacaine hydrochloride 2 mg/ml with outer label.
